About Us

Founded by two Tufts dental school graduates,
Accepted has the vision to make higher education accessible to everyone.

Christie and Christine, Tufts University alumni, united by a passion for educational equity, conceived an idea two years after they graduated: to dismantle barriers to higher education.
Combining their expertise in education and technology, they launched a platform aimed at democratizing college admissions support.
Their company, born from a desire to level the playing field, empowers students of all backgrounds to pursue academic dreams confidently.
